My Childhood Memories

I was born and raised in a small town surrounded by nature, which greatly influenced my early development. My childhood was filled with exploration, creativity, and the formation of lasting friendships.

My family played a crucial role in shaping my values and interests. My parents, both educators, instilled in me a love for learning and curiosity about the world around me.

Family Vacations

Every summer, we would take road trips to national parks. These experiences taught me to appreciate nature and sparked my interest in environmental science.

First Science Fair

In 5th grade, I built a working volcano for the school science fair. Winning first place ignited my passion for scientific exploration and experimentation.

Music Lessons

Starting piano lessons at age 7 taught me discipline and perseverance. I continued playing throughout my school years, performing in several recitals.
